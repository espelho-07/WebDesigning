Popular Indian Dishes

Explore the flavours of India through its diverse and rich culinary traditions!

North Indian Dishes

North India is known for its rich, aromatic, and flavourful dishes often cooked with ghee and spices:

  • Biryani: A fragrant rice dish made with aromatic spices and meat mixed with vegetables.
  • Butter Chicken: A creamy, tomato-based curry that's a favorite worldwide.
  • Paneer Tikka: Marinated paneer cubes grilled to perfection.

South Indian Dishes

South Indian meals offer a wide array of dishes with a perfect blend of spices and tangy flavours:

  • Masala Dosa: A crispy rice crepe stuffed with spicy potato filling.
  • Idli and Sambhar: Soft rice cakes served with a tangy lentil-based vegetable stew
  • Chettinad Chicken: A fiery, flavorful chicken dish from Tamil Nadu.

  South Indian Favourite:Massala Dosa

Sweet Delights

No Indian meal is complete without indulging in some mouth-watering desserts:

  • Gulab Jamun: Fried milk solids soaked in sugar syrup.
  • Rasgulla: Soft and spongy cheese balls in light sugar syrup.
  • Payasam: A South Indian dessert made of milk, rice, and jaggery
